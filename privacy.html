Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

NexusList ("we," "our," or "us") provides a shared list service. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture. This is used solely to identify your lists and allow sharing with other users.

2. How We Use Data

Your lists and items are stored in a real-time database to allow syncing across devices. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Deletion

Users can delete their lists at any time using the "Delete Current List" button. To request full account deletion, please contact the developer.

4. Contact

For questions regarding this policy, contact us via our website.
